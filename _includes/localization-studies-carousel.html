Enabling local businesses to enter nascent energy supply chains

DAI’s enterprise development centers provide training, upgrading, and networking competitive and enter energy supply chains.

Helping multinationals gain access to new markets and thousands of verified local suppliers

DAI’s Supplier Management Portal is a cloud-based platform that connects large-scale buyers to local small and medium-sized enterprises. Around the world, multinationals are using DAI’s proprietary platform to access thousands of verified local suppliers and measure and report on their impact on the local economy.

Supply chain and market quantification for Marine Renewables Canada

DAI assessed how to expand Nova Scotian firms’ access to foreign markets in North America, Europe, and Asia. The study developed a methodology to assess the capacity of Nova Scotian suppliers to meet the requirements of the export markets and identified market opportunities and barriers.
