Module 1

Local Content Policy, Regulation and Forecasting

Examines local content laws, regulations and policies around the world and prepares participants to forecast goods, services and workforce local content scenarios.

Module 2

Local Content Plans, Procurement and Reporting

Trains practitioners on the methods for selecting the right metrics and key performance indicators, establishing the right data collection and analysis, processes and standards, and “telling the story”.

Module 3

Local Supplier Development

Teaches the use of data and analytics to structure various types of capacity building and local content programs that are commercially effective and economically beneficial.
