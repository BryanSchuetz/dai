Arsalan oversees DAI’s projects, business development, and corporate activities in Pakistan. As a technical specialist, he focuses principally on economic growth and private sector development. His advisory work has covered economic sector appraisal, value chain analysis, market development, enterprise financial analysis, and project evaluation.

Arsalan’s client engagements have included work for the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office, the World Bank, the U.S. Agency for International Development, AusAID, the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development, and Chevron. He has worked in numerous countries in addition to Pakistan, including Jordan, Nigeria, Tanzania, and Thailand.

* B.A., business administration, finance, Institute of Business Administration-Karachi
* M.A., public policy, Brown University
